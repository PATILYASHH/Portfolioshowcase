Simple calculator: A User-Friendly Web Calculator Application

SimpleCalc" is a user-friendly web-based calculator application designed to perform basic arithmetic operations effortlessly. This intuitive calculator provides a familiar interface resembling a traditional calculator, offering functionalities for addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

SimpleCalc aims to be accessible and efficient, offering a simple solution for quick calculations without the need for complex features. Whether performing simple or slightly complex arithmetic, SimpleCalc simplifies the process, making it a handy tool for everyday calculations.
